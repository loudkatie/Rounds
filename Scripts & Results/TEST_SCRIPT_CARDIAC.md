**Key medical terms the AI should translate:**
- CABG x4 = Coronary Artery Bypass Graft (4 vessels)
- LAD = Left Anterior Descending artery (main heart artery)
- OM1 = First Obtuse Marginal (side branch)
- RCA = Right Coronary Artery
- LIMA = Left Internal Mammary Artery (best graft type)
- SVG = Saphenous Vein Graft (leg vein used for bypass)
- POD 3 = Post-operative day 3
- Extubated = Breathing tube removed
- Pressors = Medications to raise blood pressure
- Telemetry = Continuous heart monitoring
- Ectopy = Irregular heartbeats
- Mediastinal tube = Chest drain near heart
- Serous = Clear, watery fluid
- Troponins = Heart damage markers (expected high after surgery)
- EF = Ejection Fraction (heart pumping strength)
- Pericardial effusion = Fluid around heart
- CTA = CT Angiogram (scan to see blood vessels)
- PO = By mouth (per os)
- Dangled = Sitting on edge of bed with legs hanging
